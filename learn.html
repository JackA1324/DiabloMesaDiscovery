Learn

Multiple anomalies have been left unchecked due to an overflow of openings in our detected areas. It is important that these areas be addressed for the safety of everyone. The OTHER has been opened and we have found many leaks. These leaks have allowed unwanted and invasive entities into our patriotic soil. This is why we have tasked ourselves with the role of containment.

Many of our unwanted residents pose as potential safety hazards to local communities. Rather than start a panic we have decided to provide the tools necessary to keep your friends and family safe. Remember. Your safety is our priority!

Our most common entity is the walker. This entity is our priority as he can be the most deceiving. Because of this, is vital that the public learns how to spot these invasive guests

ANOMALIES

The Walker

These creatures can easily blend and pretend. If you see a person that doesn't look or feel like a person, it is most likely a walker. Trust your eyes and gut.

Hollow

These are what we hypothesize to be some form of mammal moving on four limbs standing at 7-10 ft tall. Avoid at all costs. Do not run. Do not make noise.
